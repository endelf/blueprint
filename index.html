01 — THE HEIST

CRACK THE VAULT.
LEAVE NO WITNESS.

Blueprint is a cooperative low-poly heist game where you and your crew infiltrate high-security targets — banks, corporate offices, government facilities. Coordinate your team, neutralize FBI agents, and extract with the loot before reinforcements flood the map.

Every heist is a living puzzle. Communication, positioning, and split-second decision-making separate a clean job from a bloodbath.
